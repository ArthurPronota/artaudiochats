<h1>The Art of Audio Chats</h1>

<h2>The program is designed to solve three tasks:</h2>

1. Talk to the other person in a language you don't recognize.
2. Listen in your language to news, audiobooks, videos, audio clips, audio files created in a language unknown to you.
3. Speak with your interlocutor in the same language while hiding your real voice.

You speak in a language that you know, and your interlocutor hears you in a language that he understands.
Your interlocutor speaks in a language that he knows, you hear him in your own language.

For example, you speak Russian and your interlocutor speaks English.
You will hear your interlocutor in Russian and your interlocutor will hear you in English.
You will understand each other.

Your interlocutor will hear the voice generated by the neural network, not your voice. Sometimes it's necessary.

<h2>Requirements:</h2>

* Windows 10 or Windows 11
* Available hard drive space 26 GB.
* RAM from 12 GB.
* GPU (graphics processing unit) with 6 gigabytes or more, the presence of a GPU is not necessary, but it is desirable, as the quality of work increases.
* Headphones with microphone.

One of the side applications is conversations with the interlocutor in the same language, but your interlocutor will hear the voice generated by the neural network and not your own voice. Sometimes this is necessary. There are many possible applications.

<h3>Interlocutor's chat history.</h3>

The chat history of the interlocutor is located on the right side of the browser. See Figure 16.

<img width="1788" height="899" alt="16" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/dc80b9d9-016c-4ff0-8407-02327278fe7a" />


Figure 16. Interlocutor's chat history.

The interlocutor's chat history is displayed when you click on the interlocutor in the left part

The top menu contains:

* Interlocutor's icon
* Interlocutor's nickname
* The "Start conversation" button
* The "Clear history" button
* The "Delete interlocutor" button
* The "Interlocutor's profile" button

The chat history with the selected interlocutor appears in the right part.

The chat title is numbered with a pair of yaks: "Interlocutor's language"/"Your language".

The chat title contains the "Delete this chat" button.

When you click the "Delete this chat" button, this chat is deleted from the interlocutor's chat history.

All messages are divided into 2 groups:

* Messages from the interlocutor.
* Your messages.

Messages from the interlocutor are shifted to the left. Your messages are shifted to the right.

Each message group consists of 2 parts:

* Message text in the language of the interlocutor, the color of this message is black.
* Message text in your language, the color of this message is green.

Message date corresponds to your language.

Newer messages (by date) are located lower than older ones.

At the very bottom of the interlocutor's chats is the "Start conversation" button. When you click on it, the program starts calibrating the microphone and the sound device to determine the signal/noise levels and displays the corresponding message.

After calibrating the sound devices, the names of the buttons change: "Start conversation" -> "Stop conversation".

From this point on, you can start a conversation or launch any audio/video file to listen to.

All incoming messages (from the other person) will appear on the left.

All outgoing messages (from you) will appear on the right.

You will hear messages in your language and your other person will hear messages in their language.

At the end of the conversation/listening to the video, you need to click on one of the "Stop conversation" buttons.

The conversation also ends when:

* Clicking on the other person in the left part of the browser in the list of interlocutors.
* Clicking on the "New" button at the top of the list of interlocutors.
* Clicking on the "Help" button in the top menu.
* Clicking on the "Clear history" button in the top menu.
* Clicking on the "Delete interlocutor" in the top menu.
* By clicking on the "Interlocutor's profile" button in the top menu.
* By clicking on the "Exit" button in the top menu.

The display of messages in the chat history can be adjusted by selecting the message display option in the upper left part of the menu (Green arrow).

* When selecting "All languages" - the interlocutor's messages and your messages are displayed in all languages.
* When selecting "My language" - the interlocutor's messages and your messages are displayed only in your language.
* When selecting "Interlocutor's language" - the interlocutor's messages and your messages are displayed only in the interlocutor's language.

The default message display mode is "All languages".
